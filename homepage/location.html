How to get to Brooklyn Bridge Park

There are many different ways to get to Brooklyn Bridge Park

  • Walking
  • Biking
  • Car
  • Subway- High St (A or C), York St (F), Clarck St (2 or 3), Court St (N, R or W)
  • Bus- Old Fulton St/Elizabeth Pl (B25),
  • Ferry- Dumbo/Bbp Pier 1
  • Train- PATH
